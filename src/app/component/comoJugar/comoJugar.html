How to play?

GeoQuest is a video game designed for learning geography and history in a simple and fun way, where you can progress by answering questions in different world contexts related to countries, capitals, flags, continents, and historical events.

The platform offers different game modes, which you can select before starting.

Question classification

    Each question has its own characteristics that will be chosen by the player, such as:

  • ☆ Category
  • ★ Type
  • ☆ Difficulty level

How to progress?

Each question is associated with a country, historical event, or representative element such as its flag or capital.

Users participate in matches where they answer questions by selecting the correct answer in a multiple-choice version, and they earn points based on their correct answers, which increases their ranking within the game.
